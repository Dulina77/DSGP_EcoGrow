About Us

Our Mission


EcoGrow envisions a future where urban communities actively contribute to environmental sustainability by transforming their limited living spaces into thriving hubs of organic cultivation. We aim to make urban farming an accessible and rewarding lifestyle, where every balcony and indoor corner becomes a step toward a greener, healthier, and more self-sufficient world.

Our Vision


Our mission at EcoGrow is to empower urban dwellers with intelligent, data-driven tools that promote sustainable agriculture within city environments. By combining AI-powered plant prediction, space classification, disease detection, and personalized guidance through a chatbot, we strive to simplify urban gardening. EcoGrow is dedicated to enhancing food security, promoting eco-conscious living, and fostering a deep connection between people and nature—one plant at a time.
